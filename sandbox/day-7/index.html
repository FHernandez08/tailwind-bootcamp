Day 7 - Transitions & Hove States

Topic: adding subtle motion and interaction using hover and transition utilities.

Notes (What I'm Learning Today)

  • Transitions make UI interactions feel smooth instead of abrupt.
  • transition enables animations, and duration-200 controls their speed.
  • Hover utilities like hover:bg-* and hover:-translate-y-1 add feedback.
  • Cards feel more interactive with a slight lift and shadow on hover.
  • Subtle effects feel professional; heavy animations feel distracting.
