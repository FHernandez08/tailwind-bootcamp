Day 8 - Layout Containers & Spacing

Topic: consistent containers, section spacing, and clean layout structure.

Notes (What I'm Learning Today)

  • Containers prevent content from stretching too wide on large screens.
  • max-w-5xl mx-auto centers and limits width.
  • px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8 ensures safe padding at all times.
  • Vertical spacing should be consistent using py-*.
  • Every section should follow the same layout pattern.
